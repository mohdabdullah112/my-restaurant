About us

At MyRestaurantInsurance.co.uk, we specialise in providing tailored insurance solutions for restaurants, cafes, takeaways, food trucks, catering services, and other food businesses across the UK.

With years of experience and in-depth industry knowledge, we understand the unique risks and challenges that come with running a hospitality business. From a small sandwich shop to a large multi-location restaurant, our goal is to protect your business with cover that’s reliable, affordable, and built around your needs.

We offer comprehensive policies that cover a wide range of essential protections including public liability, employers’ liability, business interruption, stock and contents, building cover, and equipment breakdown. Whether you operate a dine-in establishment, provide takeaway services, or offer outdoor seating, our team works closely with you to create a policy that ensures peace of mind. We also understand the importance of protecting against incidents like food contamination, customer injury claims, or fire and flood damage — and we’ve got you covered.

What makes us different is our commitment to personal service. We believe in being more than just a comparison site — we are your insurance partner. Our UK-based advisors are always ready to help, providing clear advice and fast support whether you're getting a quote, making a change, or submitting a claim.

Our mission is simple: to help you safeguard your business so you can focus on what matters most — running your restaurant and serving your customers. With competitive pricing, flexible cover options, and a dedicated claims team, MyRestaurantInsurance.co.uk is here to make insurance easy, transparent, and stress-free.

Specialists in commercial insurance for UK food businesses — including restaurants, takeaways, caterers, pubs, and food retailers.
